PRINCIPLES AND GOALS

1. To provide members with practical experiences that enrich their academic knowledge and skills.
2. To effectively prepare each student for their future roles as competent and competitive information technology professionals.
3. To enhance awareness and promote the effective utilization of information and communications technology among students and enthusiasts.
4. To cultivate a commitment to advanced standards of computer education within educational institutions.
5. To establish a platform for knowledge exchange among members, thereby advancing and improving ICT practices nationwide.
6. To foster collaboration and engagement among members while encouraging partnerships with other relevant organizations.
7 .To disseminate knowledge and information through a variety of organizational events and gatherings.
8. To support students in developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills through practical, hands-on activities.
9. To provide mentorship and guidance from experienced professionals in the field of information technology. 10. To encourage continuous professional development among members through workshops, seminars, and training sessions.
